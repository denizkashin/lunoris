# Lunoris

`Lunoris` is a premium fishing intelligence app that turns moon phase, solunar feeding windows, weather, barometric pressure, and geomagnetic activity into one clear **Fishing Score** from 0 to 100 — so you can decide with confidence, not guesswork.

Your journal stays on your device. Lunoris never turns your secret spots into a social feed.

## Why anglers use Lunoris

- **One number for the whole picture**  
  Excellent, good, fair, or poor conditions at your spot — readable in seconds.

- **Solunar windows that matter**  
  Major and minor feeding periods aligned with sunrise and sunset, built on John Alden Knight's model.

- **Live weather and pressure trend**  
  Stable or rising pressure vs sharp drops. Offline-first moon math; weather refreshes when you're online.

- **Best Targets today**  
  Which fish families are most likely active near you, with fishing restrictions where data is available.

- **Year-ahead calendar**  
  Plan trips up to 365 days ahead on the same scoring engine.

- **A journal that feels like a diary**  
  Active sessions, one-tap **"Caught!"** logging with GPS and an environment snapshot, photos, and a private timeline.

- **Personal insights on device**  
  Moon phases, baits, pressure bands, and success rate — computed locally from your history, not in the cloud.

- **Home screen widget**  
  Score and peak window at a glance for your saved locations.

- **Fully free**  
  Every feature unlocked. No ads. No subscription wall.

## How to use

1. Open `Lunoris`.
2. Set your home water in onboarding (GPS or map).
3. Check today's **Fishing Score** on the home screen.
4. Start a session when you head out; tap **"Caught!"** to log a catch with conditions and location.
5. Use the calendar to plan ahead and the map to revisit your spots.
